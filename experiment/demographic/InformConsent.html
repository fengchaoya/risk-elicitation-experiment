Purpose and Duration

The purpose of this experiment is to study risk attitudes under different circumstances. It consists of three sections and should take approximately 15 minutes to complete.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation in this experiment is completely voluntary, and you can withdraw at any time. We will not disclose any personally identifiable information when we present experiment research findings.

Compensation

You will be compensated for participating in this survey. Specifically:

  • Base payment: $2 for participation
  • Additional bonus: Average of $5 (ranging from $0 to $10) based on your decisions

Data Usage

Information collected for this study may be shared with other researchers and may be used for other studies. These studies may be similar to this study or different. We will ensure that your identity cannot be linked to the information we share. We will not ask for additional permission before sharing the information.

Contact Information

If you have questions about the experiment, research methods, or to report a research-related injury, please contact:

  • Dr. Elizabeth Hoffman at Iowa State University (bhoffman@iastate.edu)
  • Mr. Chaoya Feng at Iowa State University (chaoyaf@iastate.edu)

You can report any concerns about this study by calling the Compliance and Ethics Hotline at Iowa State University: (515) 294-7119

Continuing with this experiment indicates your consent to participate.
