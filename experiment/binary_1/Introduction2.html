Sample Question

In this section, you will make ten choices. Below is a sample question to help you understand the format. You do not need to answer this sample.

Which one do you prefer:
Option A
If die shows 1-3:
$5.00
If die shows 4-10:
$4.00
Option B
If die shows 1-3:
$9.63
If die shows 4-10:
$0.25
How Your Payment Will Be Determined
1
Make Your Choices: You will answer ten similar questions
2
Question Selection: We will roll the die to randomly select one of your ten decisions
3
Payment Determination: We will roll the die again to determine your payoff based on your choice
Key Point:

You will make ten decisions, but only one will ultimately affect your earnings. You will not know in advance which decision will be used.
